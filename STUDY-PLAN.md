## Daily Study Method

For every topic, follow this cycle:

```
1. READ - Go through the chapter/section
2. WRITE - Summarize in your own words (handwritten or typed)
3. SPAM QUESTIONS - Practice papers from amequestionpaper.in + AI-generated
4. REVIEW MISTAKES - Find gaps, go back to step 1 for those gaps
5. MOVE ON - Don't perfectionist-loop. One pass through the cycle, then next topic.
```
