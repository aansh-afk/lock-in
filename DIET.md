# Diet Plan - Recomp to 12-13% BF by June

## Goal

- **Body recomposition**: Lose fat, build muscle simultaneously
- **Target**: 12-13% body fat by June 2026
- **Starting point**: 68kg, 5'7", skinny fat (~18-22% BF)
- **Calories**: 2250/day (maintenance for recomp)
- **Protein**: 165g+ daily

## Vegetables (Rotate Weekly)

Pick 2 per week:
- **Broccoli** - highest protein for a vegetable, fills you up
- **Spinach** - iron, helps with energy and recovery
- **Mixed frozen vegetables** - zero prep, just heat

All take under 5 minutes. Microwave, boil, or pan.

---

## Sunday Meal Prep

### What to Do

1. **Chicken** (1.4kg for the week)
   - Marinate all at once (salt, pepper, spices of choice)
   - Bake at 200C for 25-30 min or grill in batches
   - Store in containers, 200g portions

2. **Rice**
   - Cook in bulk every 2-3 days (takes 15 min)
   - Store in fridge

3. **Vegetables**
   - Chop for the week
   - Store in containers
   - Frozen mixed veg needs zero prep

### Nightly Routine (15 min)

1. Take out tomorrow's lunch chicken from fridge
2. Cook 2 eggs
3. Heat rice (or make fresh if needed)
4. Heat vegetables
5. Pack tomorrow's lunch container
6. Eat dinner

## Rules

- **No skipping meals.** You're recomping, not cutting. Eat everything.
- **Hit 165g+ protein every day.** This is the most important number.
- **Same meals every day.** No decision fatigue. No "what should I eat."
- **Sunday prep is mandatory.** 1 hour on Sunday saves you every weeknight.
- **Water**: Minimum 3 liters per day. Carry a bottle everywhere.
