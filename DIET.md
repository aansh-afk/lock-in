## Roti Info

- 1 roti (30g atta) = ~100 cal, ~3g protein
- More filling than rice (higher fiber)
- 3 per meal = 6 per day
- Make fresh each night or batch dough for the week

---

## Vegetables (Rotate Weekly)

Pick 2 per week:
- **Broccoli** - highest protein for a vegetable, fills you up
- **Spinach** - iron, helps with energy and recovery
- **Mixed frozen vegetables** - zero prep, just heat

---

## Sunday Meal Prep

### What to Do

1. **Chicken** (1.4kg for the week)
   - Season: salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika
   - Bake at 200C for 25-30 min
   - Store in containers, 200g portions

2. **Roti dough** (optional bulk)
   - 500g atta + 250ml water + salt
   - Knead 5-8 min, store in fridge (3-4 days)

3. **Vegetables**
   - Chop for the week or buy frozen

### Nightly Routine (15 min)

1. Take prepped chicken from fridge
2. Make 3 rotis fresh on tawa
3. Cook 2 eggs
4. Heat vegetables
5. Pack tomorrow's lunch: chicken + 3 rotis + veg in container
6. Eat dinner

## Rules

- **No skipping meals.** You're recomping — eat everything planned.
- **Hit 155g+ protein every day.** This is what drives the recomp.
- **Same meals every day.** No decision fatigue.
- **Sunday prep is mandatory.**
- **Water**: Minimum 3 liters per day.
- **Don't snack outside the plan.** You're at maintenance, not surplus.
